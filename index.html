#WirVsVirus

Extraordinary times call for extraordinary innovation. Together with six other partners and supported by the German federal government, we created the #WirVsVirus 48-hour virtual hackathon to crowdsource solutions to mitigate the Covid-19 crisis. This led to several teams being supported to bring their ideas into reality through our follow-up Solution Builder support programme. Find out more about #WirVsVirus.
